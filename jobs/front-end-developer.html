About Us

HostMe's team ambitious is to become the most trusted front-of-house management system for the restaurant industry, supporting the restaurateur's vision, guiding and empowering the staff to succeed at their jobs, and making the owner's dreams come true. Our team is building a highly reliable system that can accommodate any table assignment changes in real-time without impacts on the staff's efficiency, or on the customers' online and offline experiences. The system applies machine learning technics to analyze the impact of staff decisions and to learn customer behavior, in order to provide recommendations to the managers, instructions to the hosts or hostesses, and critical business insights to the owners.

Job Description

You will join our tech team as Frontend developer and be part of guaranteeing the best user experience on all relevant devices. You will work closely with senior product, design and backend people to deliver new features and improve existing ones.

Skills & Requirements

You will need:

•  Deep knowledge about HTML(5), CSS(3), JS.
•  Experience with jQuery, Bootstrap, Haml, Less, Sass, or related libraries
•  A proven track record that shows your excellence.
•  Experience with responsive design, mobile devices.
•  Ability to work in a fast pace environment, scrum/agile team
•  The desire to bring the best user experience to our customers.
•  A get-things-done mentality and the ability to communicate and realize your ideas.
•  Plus #1: Experience with Xamarin or Cardova
•  Plus #2: Familiar with NPM, Bower, Gulp, Grant
•  Plus #3: Highly proficient in both spoken and written English
•  Plus #4: Familiar with Photoshop.
