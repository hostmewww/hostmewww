Privacy policy

HostMe is committed to safeguarding your online privacy with respect to the personally identifiable information that we may obtain from you in connection with your use of our website, www.hostmeapp.com and our associated mobile applications (the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we gather from our users and how we use it. By using or accessing the Services, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy. Capitalized terms not defined herein have the meaning set forth in our Terms of Use located at our website, www.hostmeapp.com.

The Services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. Consistent with the Federal Children's Online Privacy Protection Act of 1998, we will never knowingly gather or use personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13, and we do not allow anyone under the age of 13 to register with the Services.

Types of personally identifiable information collected.

We collect personally identifiable information from individuals and businesses using our Services, including Patrons and the restaurants that have accounts with HostMe. This information includes:

Information you provide to us. These are voluntary submissions made when you use the Services and create an Account, such as, for Patrons, your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender and, to the extent you participate in a loyalty program through our Services, the last 4 digits of your credit card used in our participating restaurants, which are provided to us during the registration process and, for restaurants, similar information for authorized representatives.

Information we collect when you use the Services. We keep track of the actions you take on the Services, including information about your restaurant visits, restaurant purchases, wait times, loyalty transactions and financial/payment information provided to us by the restaurants. Restaurants that use our Services may collect and upload information about your dining experience, including your name, dates of visits, purchases, and the last 4 digits of your credit card used for payment for purposes of rewarding customers through loyalty programs operated through the Services. By using our Services, restaurants grant us a license to use the information they upload, which may include personally identifiable information, and we will treat such information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information from your mobile device. We may access or request to access certain information stored on your mobile device including information about your location, contacts, camera and other data. In addition, we may make upgrades and changes to our App to allow you the opportunity to make payments through the Services such as through Apple iPay.

Information in connection with your Account. If you establish an Account through our Services, we may ask that you provide us with certain personal information, including your name, credit card number, expiration date, e-mail address, mailing address and telephone number. Your credit card information may be transferred to a payment processing company for processing.

Use of information collected.

Patrons. Our primary use of your information is to administer, maintain and improve your experience with our Services generally as well as provide you with customized, personalization services and interactive communications related to your dining experiences. We operate a loyalty points program through the Services in order to reward you when you visit restaurants using our Services. To operate this program, we keep track of the restaurants you visit based on your user information, including phone number. We also use this information in order to enhance and better tailor your restaurant experiences, such as by sending you text messages to update you on waiting lists, inform you of restaurant special promotions and deals, provide your name and phone number to restaurants you choose so that they can handle your reservations and wait times, and provide you with other pertinent information regarding restaurants you visit. We also use this information internally in order to help us better understand the demographics of our users. We may use some of this information for contacting you, customizing your experience on the Services including through advertising, improving our services and conducting research. We only collect personally identifiable information from you because you are voluntarily submitting the information to us in order to utilize the Services.

Restaurants. Our primary use of your information is to administer, maintain and improve your experience on our Services. We may use your financial information (e.g., your credit card number) only to verify your credit and to bill you for memberships purchased through our Services, including any Payment Information you provide, to the extent that now or in the future we process your payments through our Website or a third party payment processing company. We also use your contact information as necessary to send you information about the memberships that you have purchased. We use your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer the Services. We also use your IP address to help identify you and to gather broad demographic information that we may share with our business partners, but only in the aggregate without any of your personally identifiable information.

We may research the demographics, interests and behavior of our customers based on the information provided to us during membership registration, during sweepstakes, contests and promotions, from our server log files, from cookies and from surveys. Our research may be compiled and analyzed on an aggregate basis. We may share this aggregate data with business partners, but only in the aggregate, without any of your personally identifiable information.

We may also use your information to help us improve and enhance the Services, process transactions, resolve disputes with our users, enforce our Terms of Use, and to protect you from fraud and misuse of your information.

We may also use your contact information to send you important information such as notices required by law and updates or changes to our Terms of Use or this Privacy Policy.

Use of cookies.

Cookies enable us to customize and personalize your experience on our Website and App, including the products and promotions that are offered to you. Essentially, a cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and is stored on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies for several purposes in connection with the operation of our Website. We may use cookies to identify you and access your information stored on our computers in order to deliver you a better and more personalized experience. For example, we may use cookies to tell you about products and services specific to your interests. Upon request, we may save your "user name" so that you do not have to re-enter it every time you visit our Website. In providing you with this service, we use cookies. We may use cookies to estimate our user base and their usage patterns. Each browser accessing our Website may use a unique cookie that is then used to determine the extent of repeat visits and the user activity during those visits. We may use the historical information to help target promotions based on user interests and behavior, both specifically to individual users and on an aggregate basis with respect to all users. We also may use cookies to track your progress and number of entries in some promotions, sweepstakes and contests. For example, when a promotion uses cookies, the information coded to the cookie indicates your progress through the promotion, and may be used to track entries, submissions and status of prize drawings. Business partners that offer co-branded services and jointly-sponsored sweepstakes, contests and promotions on our Website, may use their own cookies. We have no control over those cookies, nor does this Privacy Policy cover how your personal information contained in those cookies may be used or protected. If you have any questions about the cookies of such third parties, or about the use of your personal information by such third parties, you should contact the site administrator or web-master of the third party site. You can modify your browser preferences to accept all cookies, to notify you when a cookie is set, or to reject all cookies. However, our Website uses cookie-based authentication. Accordingly, if you choose to reject all cookies, you may not be able to log onto our Website and/or use our services or participate in our sweepstakes, contests or promotions.
